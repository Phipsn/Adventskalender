Riddles and Process

The riddles are self-created and designed for teenagers and adults. They do not require any specific prior knowledge but call for clever problem-solving and online research (Internet use is allowed!). Wrapped in charming short stories featuring Mats and Roxy, the riddles present clear tasks along with subtle hints hidden between the lines. They are generally neither trivia questions nor traditional riddles or math problems.

The riddles come with short stories (about 2 A4 pages) from Mats the raccoon and Roxy the fox. The stories are designed like a children's book.
